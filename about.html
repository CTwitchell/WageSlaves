About Us

We're a group of friends who love to game together. We play on all platforms but we prefer PC.

We play a large variety of games. We play some A LOT more than others. Our favorites include: Minecraft, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Borderlands 2, Left 4 dead 1/2 and Tera

We currently have a Whitelisted private server, thanks to Chris, where we're playing the Realm Of Mianite modpack. If you're interested in playing on the server, message us and we'll give you the IP.
